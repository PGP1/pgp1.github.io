How it works

Plantly is an integrated platform which aims to bring you and your plant into ultimate synchronicity!

Plant Health Monitoring Plantly will monitor your plants health, ensuring that the optimal benchmarks are met to ensure your plant is healthy.

Predictive Machine Learning Power Through machine learning, Plantly will autonomously optimise your plants growth through semi autonomously controlling your plants biome.

Live Alerts and Notifications Plantly also offers a dashboard where you can visually view your plants health. Plantly will also send you notifications of your plant's health on the go.
